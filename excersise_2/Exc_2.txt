You can see the cubes that are made of different materials.
With key 1 you can deactivate or activate point light sources.
With key 2 you can deactivate or activate directional light sources.
With the cursor you can change the camera's view.
With E key you can move the camera up.
With Q key you can move the camera down.
With Left Shift pressed, camera's speed increases when we move it up or down.
